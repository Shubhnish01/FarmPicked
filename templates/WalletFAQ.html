Wallet FAQs


How can I add money into Farm Picked Wallet?


You may add money in Farm Picked Wallet in following ways
- Debit Cards
- Net Banking
- Any other acceptable payment methods visible on the Farm Picked payment page
However, Cash on delivery, International Cards are not acceptable modes of payment for the top-up of Farm Picked Wallet.

How much money can I add in my Farm Picked Wallet?


You can only add value up to Rs. 10,000 per month per user. Beyond that you may still receive your refunds to Farm Picked Wallet, but you will not be able to top-up it further. Also at any point in time you cannot have a balance of more than Rs 10,000 in your wallet. This is excluding any credits that you have received in this period.

What are the benefits of using Farm Picked Wallet?


- Faster Check-out by using Farm Picked Wallet, no OTP or passwords are required
- Faster refunds- these are credited within 24 hours to your Farm Picked Wallet
- Detailed account tracking: You can track your spends and wallet activity in the My Wallet link available in the Farm Picked website

What happens if I return the Items that I have bought with Farm Picked Wallet?


The refund value for these returned items shall be added to your Farm Picked Wallet.
