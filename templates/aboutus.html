What is Farm Picked


Farm Picked is Meerut's city online farm fresh product store.With over 1,000 products in our catalogue. You will find every farm item you will looking for, which includes Fresh Fruits and Fresh Vegetables. Choose from a wide range of options in both the Category, exclusively handpicked to help you find the best quality available at the lowest prices. Select a time slot for delivery and your order will be delivered right to your doorstep, anywhere in Meerut. You can pay online using Debit/Credit Card or by cash on delivery.
We guarantee on time delivery, and the best quality !
